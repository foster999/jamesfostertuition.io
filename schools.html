Individual Instrument Tuition

Syllabus

Traditional techniques and music theory are implemented and taught in a way that works with present day music. Students learn from a carefully crafted syllabus to take beginners to a Grade 1 standard within 3 terms of lessons (34 lessons) with consistant attendance and practise.
Both guitar and violin are taught to the Trinity Guildhall classical syllabus although other exam boards can be used upon request of the parents/school.

Administration and Invoicing

Enrolment forms, invoicing and collection of fees for music lessons can be time consuming and a strain on an already busy School Office. I provided all administration with regards to new students, invocing parents and collecting payment directly from parents.
